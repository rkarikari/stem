⬆ Upload to LoTW
🔐 LoTW requires QSOs to be digitally signed using TQSL (Trusted QSL software) before upload. HamLog Pro generates the correctly formatted ADIF export for direct import into TQSL.
1
Get your LoTW Certificate
Request a LoTW TLS certificate from ARRL at lotw.arrl.org. ARRL will mail a postcard to your address on record to verify your callsign. This process takes a few days for new licensees.
2
Install TQSL
Download and install TQSL (Trusted QSL) from ARRL's site. Import your LoTW certificate into TQSL. TQSL is required to sign and upload QSOs — it's free and available for Windows, Mac, and Linux.
3
Export ADIF from HamLog Pro
Select the QSOs you want to upload below, then click "Export LoTW ADIF". The file will include all required LoTW fields. You can also use the full ADIF export from the Import/Export screen.
4
Sign & Upload with TQSL
Open the exported ADIF in TQSL, sign it with your station location certificate, and upload to LoTW. TQSL will confirm successful upload.
5
Mark QSOs as Uploaded & Import Confirmations
Use the tools below to mark QSOs as sent to LoTW. When LoTW confirms QSOs, download your confirmation ADIF from lotw.arrl.org and import it here — HamLog Pro will automatically update the LoTW confirmation status for each QSO.
✅ Import LoTW Confirmations

After LoTW processes your QSOs, download your confirmation ADIF from the LoTW website and import it here. HamLog Pro will match each confirmed QSO and update its status automatically.
