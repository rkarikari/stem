RadioSport is a comprehensive STEM education program integrating amateur radio, satellite communications, drone operations, artificial intelligence, renewable energy, and advanced software development. Students gain hands-on experience with cutting-edge technologies while developing critical thinking, problem-solving, and teamwork skills.

This handbook contains 15 comprehensive chapters covering RF theory through advanced satellite operations and autonomous drone systems, plus a technical addendum on antenna system performance analysis.
